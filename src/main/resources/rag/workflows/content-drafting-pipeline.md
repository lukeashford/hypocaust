# Content Drafting Pipeline

Summary: A structured workflow for turning a brief into a publish-ready draft. It collects inputs,
expands outline, drafts sections, and performs iterative edits with tone/style constraints.

## Steps

1. Intake brief and constraints
2. Outline expansion
3. First-pass drafting
4. Targeted revision cycles (clarity, tone, SEO)
5. Final QA and packaging

## Inputs

- Brief (topic, audience, tone)
- References/links

## Outputs

- Polished draft (Markdown)
- Key points summary
